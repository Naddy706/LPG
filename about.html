ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Founded in 2005, Chirag Private Limited has grown from a regional LPG distributor to one of Pakistan’s most trusted energy companies, serving hundreds of industrial and commercial clients through Karachi Port.

OUR MISSION

To provide reliable, safe, and cost-effective LPG solutions to industries across Pakistan, ensuring uninterrupted energy supply while maintaining the highest standards of safety and environmental responsibility.

OUR VISION

To be Pakistan's most trusted and innovative LPG import and distribution company, setting industry benchmarks in operational excellence, safety standards, and customer satisfaction.
