ABOUT US

OUR STORY

Founded in 2005, Chirag Private Limited has grown from a regional LPG distributor to one of Pakistan's most trusted bulk LPG import and supply companies. With a strong focus on international sourcing and efficient logistics through Karachi Port and border trade routes, we provide reliable energy solutions to hundreds of industrial and commercial clients across Pakistan.

OUR MISSION

To provide reliable, safe, and cost-effective bulk LPG import and supply across Pakistan. We ensure uninterrupted energy delivery to industrial and commercial clients while adhering to the highest international safety and environmental standards.

OUR VISION

To be Pakistan's most trusted and innovative LPG import and supply company, setting industry benchmarks in operational efficiency, safety protocols, and customer satisfaction for industrial and commercial partners.

OUR VALUES

WHAT DRIVES US

SAFETY FIRST
